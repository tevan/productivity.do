# Creating your account

Sign up at [productivity.do/signup](/signup.html). You'll need an email
and a password. Verification email arrives within a minute — click the
link to confirm.

Once verified, you land in the calendar view. Three top-level tabs:

- **Calendar** — your events and tasks-due-today on a time grid
- **Tasks** — list or kanban board view of your to-dos
- **Notes** — long-form text, searchable

You can reorder or hide tabs in Settings → Tabs.

## What to do first

1. **Connect a calendar source.** Settings → click "Open integrations directory" — connect Google Calendar, Microsoft 365, or any CalDAV calendar (Apple, Fastmail, Proton, Yahoo). Or skip and use the native calendar.
2. **Add your timezone.** Settings → Calendar → Timezone. Important for booking pages.
3. **Try a keyboard shortcut.** Press `?` anywhere to see the full list. `T` jumps to today, `N` creates an event.
