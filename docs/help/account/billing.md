# Plans and billing

## Plans

### Free
- One user, no team features
- Native calendar, tasks, notes
- One booking page
- Connect any number of integrations
- Public API access (rate-limited)

### Pro — $12/mo, $10/mo billed annually
- Everything in Free, plus:
- Unlimited booking pages
- Custom branding (logo, color)
- AI meeting prep summaries
- Recurring events
- Priority email support

### Team — $20/user/mo, $16/user/mo billed annually
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- Round-robin booking
- Collective availability
- Team workflows
- Shared calendar sets
- Admin console (coming soon)

## How billing works

We use Stripe for payments. Settings → Account → Plan & billing.

- **Monthly** — charged on the same day each month
- **Annual** — charged once per year; ~17% discount
- **Mid-cycle upgrades** — prorated; you pay the difference for the rest
  of the period
- **Mid-cycle downgrades** — take effect at the next renewal; no refund
  for the unused portion

## Switching plans

Settings → Account → Plan & billing → "Change plan"

You can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately;
downgrades at the next renewal.

## Updating payment method

Settings → Account → Plan & billing → "Manage in Stripe"

Stripe's hosted portal lets you update card, view invoices, download
receipts.

## Canceling

Settings → Account → Plan & billing → "Cancel plan"

You keep access through the end of your billing period. After that,
you drop to the Free plan; data stays.

## Refunds

- **Monthly plans:** no refunds. Cancel before your renewal date.
- **Annual plans:** prorated refund within 30 days of charge. Email
  support@productivity.do.
- **Disputes/chargebacks:** please email us first; we resolve almost
  everything within 24 hours.

## Tax

Sales tax / VAT is calculated by Stripe at checkout based on your
billing address. Shows on every invoice.

## Receipts

Auto-emailed after each charge. Also available in Stripe's portal under
Settings → Account → Plan & billing → Manage.
