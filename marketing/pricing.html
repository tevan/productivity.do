Pricing FAQ

How does the free trial work?

Pro and Team include a 14-day trial — full features, no credit card. After the trial, your account drops to Free if you haven't subscribed; your data stays.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Nothing is deleted. Booking pages beyond the Free limit become read-only and can be re-activated when you re-subscribe. Tasks and events are unaffected.

Do you offer student or non-profit discounts?

Yes — 50% off Pro for students with a valid .edu email and registered non-profits. Email hello@productivity.do.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings — no support ticket needed. Your subscription stays active through the end of the billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Stripe. Annual plans can be paid via invoice / ACH for Team customers (50+ seats).

Is there a refund policy?

30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans, no questions asked. Monthly plans aren't refunded but you can cancel anytime to stop future charges.

Ready to consolidate your stack?

Free forever for solo use. 14-day Pro trial, no card required.
