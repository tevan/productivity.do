Frequently asked

Do I need to switch off Google Calendar?

No. Productivity is a layer on top of Google Calendar. Your events stay in Google; we sync them in real time. If you stop using Productivity tomorrow, your calendar is unchanged.

How does this compare to Calendly?

Booking pages are included in Pro at no extra cost. You get multiple meeting types, custom questions, group events, single-use invites, time polls, routing forms, branded pages, and webhook automation — basically Calendly's $20/mo Teams plan, included.

Is there an API?

Yes. /api/v1 with scoped Bearer keys, an OpenAPI 3.1 spec, and signed outbound webhooks. Free on every plan including the free tier. Read the docs.

Can I host this myself?

The backend will be open-sourced. Self-hosting will be supported on Pro and Team plans, with a license that lets you run it on your own infrastructure.

What about my privacy?

We don't sell data, train AI on your calendar, or share with third parties. Read the privacy policy and security overview.
