Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We aim for plain language. Email privacy@productivity.do if anything is unclear.

What we collect

  • Account data: email, password hash, plan, billing info (handled by Stripe — we don't see card numbers).
  • Calendar data: events synced from Google Calendar, including titles, descriptions, attendees, locations.
  • Tasks: synced from Todoist (title, description, due date, project, labels).
  • Booking data: bookings made on your booking pages, including invitee name, email, phone (if required), and answers to custom questions.
  • Usage logs: timestamps, IP addresses (for rate limiting), API request paths. Retained 30 days.
  • Cookies: a single signed session cookie. No tracking pixels, no third-party analytics.

How we use it

  • To provide the Service (sync your calendar, deliver booking confirmations, etc.).
  • To communicate with you about service issues, updates, and product changes.
  • To investigate abuse or security incidents.
  • To bill you for paid plans (via Stripe).

What we don't do

  • We don't sell, rent, or trade your data.
  • We don't use your calendar or task content to train AI models.
  • We don't run third-party advertising or behavioral tracking.
  • We don't share data with third parties except the integrations you explicitly enable (Google, Todoist, Stripe).

Third-party processors

We use these vendors strictly to deliver the Service:

  • Stripe — billing and subscription management.
  • Resend — transactional emails (confirmations, reminders).
  • Cloudflare — DNS, CDN, DDoS protection.
  • Google — only for users who connect Google Calendar; we use the standard OAuth2 flow with read/write scopes.
  • Todoist — only for users who connect Todoist.

Data retention

  • Active account data: retained for the life of your account.
  • Deleted accounts: data permanently removed within 30 days.
  • Backups: 90 days, encrypted, used only for disaster recovery.
  • Logs: 30 days.
  • Webhook delivery records: 30 days.

Your rights

Regardless of jurisdiction, you can:

  • Access: export all your data via the account settings page.
  • Correct: edit any of your data through the app.
  • Delete: permanently remove your account from settings.
  • Object: opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Port: download your data as JSON, ICS, or CSV.

EU/UK customers (GDPR)

You have all the rights above plus the right to file a complaint with your supervisory authority. Our lawful basis for processing is contract performance (delivering the Service you signed up for).

California customers (CCPA)

You have the same rights as listed above. We do not sell personal information as defined by CCPA.

Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 16. If we learn we've collected data from someone under 16, we'll delete it promptly.

Security

See our security overview for technical detail. Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), bcrypt password hashes, sha256 API key hashes, encrypted OAuth tokens at rest.

Changes to this policy

Material changes are announced with 30 days' notice via email and on this page.

Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@productivity.do. Security disclosures: security@productivity.do.
